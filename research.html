Research Areas & Publications

My primary research interests lie at the intersection of Fractal Geometry, Dynamical Systems, and Ergodic Theory. Specifically, I investigate properties like the Hausdorff dimension of self-similar sets, transformations preserving specific measures, and convergence behaviors.

A core sample of the mathematical frameworks I work with involves analyzing ergodic averages: $$\lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{1}{n} \sum_{k=0}^{n-1} f(T^k x) = \int_{\Omega} f \, d\mu$$
