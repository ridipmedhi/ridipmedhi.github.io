About Me

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher in Mathematics specializing in the structural and statistical behaviors of dynamical systems. I recently completed my Ph.D. in Mathematics at the Indian Institute of Technology Delhi.

My academic focus centers on exploring complexities within geometric structures, invariant measures, and chaotic systems. I am particularly drawn to problems that bridge abstract analysis with concrete geometric representations.

My work investigates how deterministic rules can generate random-like complexity, applying tools from ergodic theory and fractal geometry to understand the stability and distribution of orbits in complex spaces.
